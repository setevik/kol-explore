### 🐉 The Bonerdagon — DEFEATED... by me losing
With Haert open, I went to fight **The Bonerdagon** (final Cyrpt boss):

> As you enter the Haert of the Cyrpt, you hear a squawking roar, followed by a roaring squawk. This huge creature rises from a pile of bones, flaps its wings, and advances toward you.

Critical lessons learned:
- **He hits for 46–49 spooky damage every turn**
- **He interrupts spells with wing-flap** — Stuffed Mortar Shell, Lasagna Bandages, even Entangling Noodles all got cancelled mid-cast multiple times
- **Cannelloni Cannon (8 MP, direct damage)** got through and dealt 42 damage in one hit — the best skill against him
- I tanked one full hit (49 spooky CRIT) but couldn't sustain through repeated 46-dmg blasts. Lasagna Bandages couldn't even land due to interrupts.

Result: **Lost the fight, ran away at HP 1**, took Beaten Up debuff.

**Strategy notes for next attempt:**
- More base HP (Crown of the Goblin King + max-power gear)
- Stack **Smothers's Blessing** + maybe a second spooky-resist source
- Open with **Knob Goblin firecracker** items (combat items can't be interrupted by wing-flap)
- I have 72 firecrackers; that's the key
- Or skip Bonerdagon for now and come back at higher level

Mine non-combats:

  - Give 'im the stick → ?

- **"More Locker Than Morlock"** → **7-Foot Dwarven mattock** (the weapon, also lets you mine ore)

Once I had all 3 pieces, I equipped the **"Mining Gear"** outfit and the next mine adventure put me into a **mining mini-game**:

> Inside of Itznotyerzitz Mine
> INSTRUCTIONS: Starting at the bottom of the mine, click a chunk of rock adjacent to an open section of mine to excavate that space.

It's an 8-column × 8-row grid:
- **Open Cavern** tiles (already mined, white/dark)
- **Rocky Wall** (gray, normal rock — clicking gives nothing or cave-in)
- **Promising Chunk of Wall** (sparkly variants — these have ore/loot!)

Sparkle variants seem to indicate different rewards:
- sparkle1, 2, 3, 5 — different ore/Meat/items I think (TBD)

Each click is 1 adv. Adjacent to open cavern = safe mine. Wrong adjacency = **cave-in** for ~43-46 damage.

**Mining results so far:**
- (2,6) Promising → 3 meat stacks (300 Meat)
- (4,6) Promising → 3 meat stacks (300 Meat)
- (5,6) Promising → **stone of eXtreme power**
- (4,5) Promising → cave-in (-46 HP)

Got 6 meat stacks worth of Meat (~600) but **no asbestos ore yet**. Will need more Mining adventures tomorrow.
