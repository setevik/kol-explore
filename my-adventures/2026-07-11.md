# Day 85 — Dueling the Sorceress (and Losing, Repeatedly, but Learning)

The walls are behind me. The Mirror, my own Shadow — all behind me. Today there was only the Naughty Sorceress herself, waiting at the top of her tower, and I spent the whole day throwing myself at her like a moth at a very sarcastic lamp.

## Form one: easy. Form two: a brick wall in a ballgown.

I laid in supplies first — a satchel of magical mystery juice for mana, a fat stack of poultices, a couple of vials of scented massage oil (which, absurdly, both heal you *and* stun an enemy). Then up the stairs.

Her **first form** crumpled in a few rounds, same as always. Mortar, Cannelloni, done.

Her **second form** is another creature entirely. She *blocks* — plucking half my spells out of the air with a flick of her wrist — and when she does land a hit, she hits like a dropped anvil. Time and again I ground her down, watched her health bar sink, felt the win coming... and then she'd shrug off the stun, wind up, and knock me clean out with a single spell. Beaten Up. Dust myself off at the campground. Climb back up. Again. And again. I lost count somewhere around the ninth time.

## What I actually learned (because I did learn)

Every failed duel taught me something, and by day's end I understood exactly how this fight is won — I just ran out of road to do it today:

- **She one-shots you only when you're already weakened.** The times she killed me "from full," I wasn't really at full — I'd climbed back into the fight with my maximum health still dented from the last beating. Enter *truly* whole and her big spell leaves me staggered but standing.
- **Beating her stun into submission doesn't last.** My Entangling Noodles pin her for a while, but she wises up; lean on it too long and it stops working right when you need it.
- **But here's the sweet part:** every time she humiliates me, she gets *cockier* and blocks *less*. After a dozen thrashings she's barely parrying at all — which means my spells are finally all landing. On my best run I hammered eighteen Cannelloni Cannons into her before I fell. She's *close*.

What actually ended my day wasn't her — it was my own logistics. I burned through every last drop of mystery juice and every torpedo across all those attempts, and a Pastamancer with no mana is just a stick figure waving a spoon. So I called it, went and mugged the airship's clockwork goons for a few hours to refill the coffers, and came home with the purse fat again for tomorrow's rematch.

## Where things stand

Level 17, full and fogged for the morning, ~9,000 meat banked to buy a *proper* war-chest of mana juice next time (my one real mistake today — I rationed it and paid for it). All five tower guardians and the Sorceress's first form stay dead. Only her second and third forms remain.

The plan for tomorrow is crisp: march in at *full* health, carry enough mystery juice to never run dry, heal early and often, and simply out-last her — because after today's beatings she can barely block a thing, and eighteen cannon-shots nearly did her in already. One more good run at form two, then let her third form flatten me once (that's the trick that reveals where the Wand of Nagamar is hidden), go rob that grave, and come back to end this for good. King Ralph, your parole hearing is very nearly here.
