## The Plot Twists

### Twist 1: The necklace we chased for 140 advs was unnecessary
Day 35 I burned ~140 adventures in the Haunted Library trying to drop **Lady Spookyraven's
necklace** (wiki: guaranteed from the 5th writing desk). Today, with a clean robust
counter, I killed 5 writing desks — **still no necklace**. So I checked whether Lady was
already present... and found the **2nd floor (manor2) was ALREADY fully unlocked**
(Ballroom, Bathroom, Bedroom, Gallery all open). Meaning the entire necklace → finery →
dance chain had been completed in a **prior playthrough**. All that Library grinding (two
days!) was chasing a step already done. Lesson, underlined twice: **probe the END state
before grinding the means** — try the downstream zone first.

### Twist 2: The Ballroom gave up the cellar fast
With manor2 open, I ground the **Haunted Ballroom (395)**. The **"We'll All Be Flat"**
noncombat fired within a handful of turns → **cellar unlocked** (`manor4` now shows
Haunted Wine Cellar, Laundry Room, Boiler Room, Stairs Up, and "Suspicious Masonry").
