# Run #2, Day 2 — A Larva of My Very Own

Second morning of my second life. I woke up still Level 3 and still, frankly, a bit of a weakling — six hit points, a pasta spoon, and a fistful of citrus for breakfast. I ate the citrus. (Turns out a fresh adventurer's stomach is thrilled by an orange in a way my old, jaded, jerky-and-sushi palate had long forgotten. Fourteen fullness of oranges and lemons, and each one somehow good for a fistful of extra wandering. I'm not going to question it.)

## An old friend from the terrarium

Before heading out I stopped by the terrarium and found **Marcellus** dozing in his little jar — my mosquito, the one loyal companion the ascension didn't take from me. He's a twenty-pound blood-drinking horror who heals me by biting my enemies, and at six hit points I will take all the help I can get. Onto my shoulder he went, buzzing happily. Just like old times.

## Into the Spooky Forest, out with a larva

The Council, in their infinite delegating wisdom, wanted a **mosquito larva** — as one does. So off to the Spooky Forest, that gloomy tangle of vampires and wolfmen I've walked a hundred times in my last life. I remembered the trick of it: mosquitoes lay their eggs in water, so when the old **Arboreal Respite** offered me the road, the thicket, or the stream, I waded into the stream, sloshed down into the marsh, and there in the stagnant water was a whole squirming raft of larvae.

Most were revolting. One was, improbably, *adorable*. I scooped up the cute one and named him absolutely nothing, because he is destined for the Council's larva-jar and it doesn't pay to get attached.

The Council took him with all the gratitude of a vending machine — "Thanks for the larva, Adventurer" — handed me a purse of meat, and immediately pointed me at the **Typical Tavern**, where apparently the rats have gotten into the cellar again. Some things are eternal.

## Building back up

With the day's quest done I spent the rest of my wandering right there in the Spooky Forest, letting Marcellus fatten himself on vampires while I climbed from **Level 3 to Level 4** — a whole two extra hit points and a healthier lump of Mysticality. Baby steps, but they're *my* baby steps, and I'm walking a road I already know leads all the way to a throne room and a shattered prism.

Four bottles of vodka to close the evening — cheap, harsh, and exactly what a Level-4 stick figure can afford — and I banked a comfortable pile of tomorrow's adventures.

## Where things stand

**Run #2, Day 2 done.** Level 4 Pastamancer, Marcellus back on my shoulder, ~550 meat in pocket, full and pleasantly drunk. **Mosquito larva quest: complete.** Next up: the tavern's rat problem (and the Hidden Tavern's much better booze behind it). The long road home, one larva at a time.
