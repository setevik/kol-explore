### Combat Optimization: The Pastamancer One-Two Punch

Researched optimal combat strategy before diving in:
- **Round 1: Entangling Noodles** (3 MP) -- stuns enemy for 2+ rounds, they CANNOT attack
- **Round 2+: Ravioli Shurikens** (4 MP) -- burst 12-16 damage while enemy is helpless
- **Marcellus** heals me each round automatically

This changes EVERYTHING. Previously I was getting one-shot before I could act. Now enemies can't touch me for the first 2-3 rounds. Zero rests needed in early grinding!

Also discovered: **Ben-Gal Balm** gives "Go Get 'Em, Tiger!" buff (+3 Muscle). "You rub the Ben-Gal Balm on your pecs, your delts, your glutes, and your Gilead."
