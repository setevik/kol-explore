## The Apartment teach-moment

Started Day 44 on the Apartment (Thrice-Cursed plan). Got the **Action Elevator** NC at
turn 9, took **option 2 "Go to the mezzanine"** → Once-Cursed (duration 10 advs). Two
fights later the effect was simply gone. The wiki is technically right that the duration
counts down per *adventure*, but **KoL counts each combat round as an "adventure"** for
effect tickdown — a long fight burns 4+ rounds, so a "10 Adventures" debuff often dies
inside 2–3 zone clicks. Without a fast-stack source (rare pygmy shaman or Cursed Punch),
the Apartment is a no-go on this build today. Filed for later when the Hidden Tavern is
unlocked.
