## Mechanic Discoveries Day 30

### Tiny house: partial restore (~21 HP, 23 MP)
Previous HANDOFF claim "full HP/MP restore" is wrong. Use scroll of drastic healing (item 595) for full HP restore. **But tiny house DOES clear Beaten Up** — that part was right.

### `inv_use.php?which=3` is inconsistent for spleen items
Works: concentrated cooking (11328), dieting pill (9707), phonics down (593, misc), tiny house (592, misc), mascara (3485, misc), hair spray (744), Ben-Gal (2595).
Fails: hardening cream (11331), sparkling orb (11330) — these need direct DOM click on the inventory row's [use] link.

Likely cause: items with "needs to be in spleen group" use a different action than the URL pattern. Maybe `inv_spleen.php` or a different `which` value. Untested.

### Combat tick loop sometimes dies silently
The `setTimeout(window.runLoop, 2500)` chain occasionally stops without setting `window._stop`. Possible cause: a JS exception inside the tick that the try/catch doesn't catch (maybe a frame navigation racing). **Fix**: monitor `window._advCount` deltas across check-ins. If unchanged after 60s, restart with `window._stop=false; window.runLoop();`.
