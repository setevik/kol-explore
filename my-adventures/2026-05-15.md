## The MP Economy Wall

Top Floor giants are **tanky**. The Mortar+Cannelloni combo (16 MP/fight) kills in 2 rounds but drains 186 MP in ~11 fights. Once MP-starved:
- Spaghetti Spear (1 MP, ~2-3 dmg) + Marcellus (~22 dmg + heal) + Ned (~15 dmg + heal) still WINS every fight, but takes **6-12 rounds** each
- Net: sustainable but ~3 min/adv vs ~1 min/adv when MP-flush

**phonics down via `inv_use.php?which=3` only applies the Tingly buff — it does NOT restore the +99 MP via URL.** (Same silent-fail class as spleen items.) MP restoration options that actually work: campground rest (1 adv, +10 MP), tiny house DOM-click (~23 MP). Neither scales well.

Loop was upgraded to fall back to **weapon Attack** at 0 MP (instead of stalling) and to treat "Not in a Fight" as a continue-signal — makes it disconnect-robust.

## The Disconnect Gauntlet ⚠️⚠️

**This session was dominated by Chrome-extension disconnects** — roughly one every 8-12 minutes. Each cycle cost ~10-15 min and netted only 1-3 advs:
1. Extension drops mid-loop
2. Often the KoL session also expires → bounced to login.php
3. Re-login (ClaudeCode/REDACTED)
4. If page reloaded, `window.tick`/`runLoop` are wiped → full reinstall
5. Often left mid-fight with 0 MP → finish via weapon-attack mini-loop
6. Restart loop... disconnect again

Suspected cause: **Chrome's extension service worker sleeps when Chrome is backgrounded.** Mitigation for future sessions: keep Chrome focused/foreground, or expect ~3 advs per reconnect and plan shorter monitoring windows (~270s, cache-warm).
