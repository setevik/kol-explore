## The Hidden City Entry — the REAL mechanic (hard-won)

I burned ~40 advs on a wrong theory (waiting for a "hemisphere" door) before the wiki
set me straight. The correct, verified sequence:

1. **Get the Nostril of the Serpent** (Day 40) — Such Great Heights → climb vines.
2. In the **Hidden Heart of the Hidden Temple** (choice 580), take **opt 2 "Go down the
   stairs"** (NOT the door!). With the Nostril in inventory, the Nostril clicks into a
   framework and the room becomes **"Unconfusing Buttons"** (choice 584).
3. **Unconfusing Buttons**: press **opt 4 "the cute little lightning-tailed guy"**
   (Pikachutlotal). This rumbles the temple and reconfigures the Hidden Heart door to
   the lightning-tailed (Pikachutlotal) variant — permanently.
4. Back at **Hidden Heart**, the door is now Pikachutlotal → **opt 1 "Go through the
   door" (3 Adventures)**.
5. **Trap room sequence** (Indiana Jones gauntlet):
   - **"At Least It's Not Full Of Trash"** (choice 123, closing walls): **opt 2 "Raise
     your hands up toward the heavens"** (a bar lowers; grab it). *Wrong answers = ~145
     dmg / Beaten Up — I learned this the hard way by kneeling first.*
   - **Spelling tiles**: walk up the tile grid spelling **"BANANAS"** (b-a-n-a-n-a-s),
     one tile per row, picking the matching letter each row. Wrong tile = instant death.
     (Tile letters decode from the image filename: `tileX.gif` → letter X.)
   - **"No Visible Means of Support"** (choice 125): **opt 3 "Do nothing"** → ceiling
     collapses safely → climb to the roof → **Hidden City revealed.**

**Door-decoration RNG was a red herring** — gargoyle/hemisphere doors are other content;
the lightning-tailed door is forced by the button, not by waiting on RNG. The whole
"grind for the right door" approach (Days 40-42 early) was wasted; the stairs→buttons
route is deterministic.
