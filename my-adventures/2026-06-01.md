## Mechanics learned (filed for tomorrow)

- **Copperhead Club = snarf 383** (in Wrong Side of the Tracks: `place.php?whichplace=town_wrong`).
- **Shen's artifact rotates per ascension-day** — fixed once the quest starts. Today's: **Eye of the Stars / Hole in the Sky / Snake With Like Ten Heads.**
- **Hole in the Sky = snarfblat 83** (CORRECTED — not 84). Needs steam-powered model
  rocketship in inventory (passive — held, not consumed). **No daily cap** — adventure
  freely until you get the Snake With Like Ten Heads (then Eye of the Stars drops).
- **"You shouldn't be here." (no "yet", no brave-bypass button)** means **wrong zone /
  not unlocked**, NOT a daily cap. The wiki "shouldn't be here YET" message is the
  stat-warning variant that DOES have a brave-or-foolish bypass.
- **Steam-powered model rocketship** drops from the **Copper Feel** NC at Castle Top Floor (324), option 2 "Investigate the Whirligigs and Gimcrackery." High rate (first try today).
- **Red Zeppelin Mooring** = `place.php?whichplace=zeppelin`. Protester mob at snarf 384. "Fire Up Above" NC opt 1 = clear 3 protesters/NC.
- **Forced-choice NCs persist across days/sessions.** If you ended the previous day mid-choice (Pantry Trespasser was today's offender), resolve it before any other action — all your navigations silently bounce until you do.
- **Snake With Like Ten Heads is a hard hit** (~70+ dmg/round) — enter at FULL HP and
  burst with Cannelloni R1 → Mortar R2 → Cannelloni R3. Skip Lasagna — it just wastes a
  damage round that you need for the kill. Today's Snake fight at HP 69 start → Lasagna
  at HP 30 → BU (snake won).
