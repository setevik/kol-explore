### The Equipment Re-Audit (per your feedback)
Before grinding, I reconsidered gear for a Pastamancer (myst-based spell-caster):
- **Hat**: Kept **Crown of the Goblin King** (Power 70) — best raw power, gives HP/MP
- **Weapon**: Swapped pool cue → **pasta spoon** (Pastamancer class item, likely spell-buff enchantments)
- **Pants**: Tried **ninja hot pants** (Power 70 > Uberpants 45) but stats DROPPED — `ninja hot pants` lack class-relevant enchantments. Reverted to **Knob Goblin Uberpants** (Power 45 but +Muscle/+Myst enchantments).
- **Accessory**: Swapped Pine-Fresh → **ring of half-assed regeneration** (HP+MP regen in combat, huge for spell-spamming Pastamancer)

**Lesson**: raw Power ≠ always better. A lower-Power item with a class-appropriate enchantment beats a higher-Power generic piece. The ninja hot pants test proved it.

### 👩‍🎨 Bathroom — The Cosmetics Wraith
The Bathroom had normal fights against "stacks of tissue paper forming a human-shaped monstrosity" — easy. Also a **"Lights Out in the Bathroom"** semi-rare (clock strikes 13) with fun flavor (smeared "World's Fynest Lead-Based Lip Coloure" on like a clown).

The puff encounter: **"Never Gonna Make You Up"** — an ornate monogrammed makeup case bouncing like it's possessed. I opened it and triggered a boss fight with a **cosmetics wraith**. It hit me for 33 dmg (HP 16/49), but Entangling → Shurikens burst cleared it:

### 👠 Gallery — The Art History Puzzle
Gallery was the most involved. First I got the **"Out in the Garden"** painting (knights + maidens, walking into a Renaissance garden). I talked to the maidens and got the effect **Dreams and Lights (10 advs)** — a huge buff that **reduces MP costs by ~75%** while in the Gallery:
- Ravioli Shurikens: 4 MP → **1 MP**
- Lasagna Bandages: 6 MP → **3 MP**

This made Gallery grinding very efficient. Got another "Lights Out" semi-rare (Magritte apple painting), and a **"pitchfork couple"** combat that's immune to Entangling Noodles (grindStep needed fixing to detect "don't have that skill" responses and fall back to Shurikens).

Finally hit **"Louvre It or Leave It"** — an M.C. Escher lithograph staircase maze. Stepped into the painting, then:
- Walked into a **Dalí** desert (melting clocks, drooping branches)
- Chose "southeast stairs" → stepped into a **Degas** ballerina studio → pilfered shoes from a little girl whose feet matched Lady Spookyraven's size

👠 **You acquire an item: Lady Spookyraven's dancing shoes** ✅

### What Worked
- **Equipment re-audit** per your suggestion was crucial. Pasta spoon + regen ring improved sustain vs the pool cue (which only mattered for the already-defeated Billiards boss).
- **Dreams and Lights effect** in the Gallery cut MP costs by ~75% — absolutely broken for sustained grinding.
- **Cyrpt Alcove** is way safer than the Nook. Zmobies do physical damage; skeleltons do spooky. Match your blessings to your zone.
- The **wet coffin brain** is almost certainly a Cyrpt puzzle item. Save it.

### What Didn't Work
- **Ninja hot pants** for a Pastamancer — wrong enchantments. Always read the enchantment text, don't just look at Power.
- **Drinking to exactly 14** — missed by 2 overshooting to 16. Lesson: plan drunkenness in single-point bumps via Imp Ale at the end, don't save the cocktails (2-drunk each) for last.
- **Pitchfork couple in Gallery** — Entangling Noodles doesn't work ("You don't have that skill!"). Multi-enemy fights may be immune to the stun. Updated the grindStep to detect this and fall back to Shurikens.
