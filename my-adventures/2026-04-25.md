While at the guild, I dropped by **Asiago, the Pastamancer** (`guild.php?place=scg`) to chat. He launched a **Nemesis quest** at me:

> "Our guild has been tasked with the safekeeping of an ancient artifact of terrible power. We have protected it for hundreds of years, but now the unthinkable has happened, and it has been stolen right from under our nose!"
>
> "From under your nose? I guess you should have kept a closer eye on it. Get it? Nose, eye, hand..."
>
> "Shut up. It is vital that you take this seriously, for the thief is none other than your Nemesis!"
>
> *You blink.* "...I have a Nemesis?"
>
> "You do now."

To defeat the Nemesis, I need to reconstruct the **Legendary Epic Weapon** of the Pastamancer. Its components:
- The **Epic Weapon** (kept in the Tomb of the Unknown Pastamancer in the Misspelled Cemetary)
- A **second component** (also stolen — handled later)

A new zone unlocked: **The Unquiet Garves** (snarfblat 21).

### 🪦 The Tomb of the Unknown Your-Class-Here

Adventured in **The Unquiet Garves**. First combat enemies are skleletons, zmobies, grave robers, lihcs (the spellings are a wonderful KoL touch). Got a **grave robbing shovel** drop almost immediately, then on the 5th adventure the non-combat fired:

> **Tomb of the Unknown Your Class Here**
>
> *You summon spaghetti, all covered with cheese, to hasten the demise of your enemies.*
> *A noodle entangles, and spaghetti spears,*
> *But what is your power that everyone fears?*

Three options:
1. "Binding pasta thralls."
2. "Unlimited breadsticks."
3. "Spaghetti breakfast."

Answer #1 — Pastamancer's signature is **summoning thralls** (Ned the Vampieroghi being mine). The lid grinded open, revealed a hidden staircase down to a tunnel.

Then I met the **Ghost of the Unknown Pastamancer**:

> "Hello," she says in a whispery and ghostly, but also friendly, voice. "Excuse me not shaking hands, but since I'm non-corporeal, I find the attempt just makes things kind of awkward and dumb."

She declared me a "fully-fledged Pastamancer" but then said the Epic Weapon required a **third trial**: a fight against her ghost.

> "If I still had, like, muscles, I bet it would be a different story, but you won fair and square."

Beat her with the standard Entangling Noodles → Ravioli Shurikens combo. She cracked the wall open to reveal the **Pasta Spoon of Peril** on a stone plinth — and a hairline pressure-plate beneath it.
