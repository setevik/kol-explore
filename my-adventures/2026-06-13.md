### 4. Fixing the economy (the actual lesson)
The war runs on MP, MP runs on MMJ, MMJ runs on meat, and the battlefield pays in *peanuts*. So:
- **Farmed the Penultimate Fantasy Airship (snarf 81)** — ~67 meat/fight NET (Cannelloni one-shots everything, zero Beaten-Ups). Rebuilt the war chest.
- Upgraded the combat engine to **v5**: full-HP entries via out-of-combat **Lasagna Bandages** (skill 3009 via `runskillz.php`, ~16 HP/cast — *works out of combat!*), Cannelloni-first bursts, periodic Springy Fusilli for initiative, and **auto-buying MMJ from surplus meat**.
- Result: the funded battlefield runs were clean — **~33 frat-boy kills total, 0 Beaten-Ups** once the MP flowed.

### 5. Wrap (EAT → ADVENTURE → DRINK, by the book)
- **DRINK problem:** I had *zero* booze and all three Hidden Tavern drinks (Fog Murderer / Cursed Punch / Bowl of Scorpions) cost **500 meat each.** Scrounged a Mad Train wine (+3) and an unnamed cocktail (+2) from inventory → drunk 5, and they handed back **7 adventures**.
- Re-farmed the Airship → afforded **1 Fog Murderer (500 meat)** → +6 drunk (**11/14**) and **+14 more adventures.** Booze that pays you back is the best kind.
- **🍖 Discovery:** *unidentified jerky (item 2620)* = **+2 fullness / +8 adventures.** I had **48** of these sitting in my bag the whole time, ignored, while I ate inferior sushi. Topped food to **14/15** with one jerky.
- Spent the bonus advs across the battlefield and a final meat-cushion farm.
