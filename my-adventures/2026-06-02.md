## 🧩 Mechanics nailed down this session

- **Mall buying via fetch**: the store buy form's `whichitem` is a composite
  `itemid.price` (e.g. tiny house = `592.214`). Buy with
  `mallstore.php?whichstore=STORE&buying=Yep.&whichitem=ID.PRICE&quantity=N&pwd=`.
  Cheapest tiny-house store this session = 1053259 @ 214 ea.
- **MMJ restock**: `shop.php?whichshop=guildstore2&action=buyitem&whichrow=527&quantity=N`
  (100 Meat each). Burns FAST — ~0.8–1 MMJ per fight. Buy in bulk (40+).
- **Red Zeppelin = snarf 385**; Ron appears as the zone boss after ~15 regular fights.
- **talisman o' Namsilat (item 486)** = meat-paste **Copperhead Charm (7178, Ron's drop)**
  + **Copperhead Charm (rampant) (7186, Shen's)** via
  `craft.php?mode=combine&action=craft&a=7178&b=7186&qty=1&pwd=`. Then **EQUIP it**
  (accessory). **Equip needs a free accessory slot** — had to unequip the imp unity ring
  first (`inv_equip.php?which=2&action=unequip&type=acc1`).
- **Palindome = snarf 386**, reached via `place.php?whichplace=plains` (only shows the
  Palindome link while the talisman is equipped). `place.php?whichplace=palindome` always
  says "not accessible" even when unlocked — go via the Plains.
- **Palindome photo NCs**: choice **129 "Do Geese See God?"** opt 1 = photograph of God
  (500m); choice **873 "Rod Nevada, Vendor"** opt 1 = photograph of a red nugget (500m).
  Photos of a **dog** and an **ostrich egg** drop from Palindome monsters (no NC).
- **Rapid fetch loops can force a logout** — one logout early in the protester grind.
  Adding a ~150ms `sleep` between adventures stopped it recurring. Re-login wipes all
  `window.*` helper globals; reinstall them after any navigation.
- **Palindome monsters** have palindrome names (Evil Olive, etc.), are spell-vulnerable,
  but a touch tankier (~5 rounds with Cannelloni). No BU issues with the stun combo.
