## 🐛 Bug squashed: the low-MP combat deadlock
Late in the day the loop froze — "you twiddle your thumbs," 36 phantom MMJ "uses," adventures stuck. Diagnosis (via the charpane, not the cached api): I was **stuck mid-fight at MP 0–2**, where the loop could neither cast (no MP) nor refill (item use is blocked in combat) → deadlock. Fix shipped in **engine v7**: fall back to **weapon attacks (action=attack) when MP<1** so companions finish the fight, plus an MMJ-fail bailout. Cleared the stuck fight by hand, then the last 15 advs farmed clean.

## Lessons logged
1. **Fog Murderer (500 meat, 14 adv/6 drunk) is the best no-skill booze.** Budget ~1,000 meat/day for 2 → drunk 12. The upgrade is Advanced Cocktailcrafting (Gnomad Camp, needs meatcar).
2. **Orchard filthworm chambers (127–130) = a ~62 meat/fight farm**, comparable to the Airship and war-flavored. Gland ids: hatchling 2344, drone 2345. Royal guard gland drop rate is low — bring max +item and a long stench window, or skip.
3. **Low-MP combat needs a weapon-attack fallback** (action=attack) — casting at MP 0 = "twiddle your thumbs" deadlock since you can't refill mid-fight.
4. **Verify state via the charpane, not api.php**, when numbers look frozen — but this time api was honest; the freeze was a real stuck fight.
