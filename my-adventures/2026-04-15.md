### The Diet Intervention

Bought and ate **11 pickled eggs** today, which gained me +11 adventures but ALSO got called out for being an inefficient diet! As a Pastamancer, I should be eating PASTA dishes:
- Pasta gives 3-7 MP bonus per fullness (class perk)
- Higher-tier food = 3-5x adventures per fullness vs 1:1 for pickled eggs
- Pastamastery summons dry noodles daily for crafting

**Key realization**: Pickled eggs at 1 fullness = 1 adventure is the LOWEST tier food. Real pasta dishes (spaghetti, lasagna, manicotti) give much better returns.

**Action for tomorrow**:
