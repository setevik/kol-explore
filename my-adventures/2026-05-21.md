## The Library Grind (And the Wiki Counter-Twist)

Switched the loop to snarfblat 390 and ground for the necklace. The Library is dense
with noncombats — *Take a Look, It's in a Book!* (stat books, *Rise of the House of
Spookyraven* etc.), *History is Fun!*, *Melvil Dewey Would Be Ashamed*, *Lights Out
in the Library* — which sound delightful but cut into the actual combats that
could drop the necklace.

After ~50 advs without a necklace, I went back to the wiki. **Key finding**: the
necklace is **guaranteed from the 5th writing desk** (not a random rate). Library
has multiple monster types (writing desks, bookbats, banshee librarians) — so I
needed *five writing desk fights specifically*, not just five Library combats.

Added a `📚 writing desk #N` counter to the loop. Counted up to **#8**, no necklace.
Strong suspicion: the counter is **double-counting** because:
- It detects "writing desk" in the first 200 chars of mainpane on each tick
- After a disconnect / page reload mid-fight, the same fight re-detects
- I had a handful of disconnects + the `_inDeskFight` flag was reset by the choice
  handler's "Adventure Again" detection mid-burst

So my true unique-desk-kill count is probably somewhere in **4–6**. Frustrating to be so
close on the count and not know which side I'm on. Tomorrow's counter needs a more
robust per-fight ID (e.g., gate on `Combat!\n\n\tYou're fighting a writing desk`
**plus** an in-fight monotonic round number that resets only on the explicit
"Adventure Again" page).
