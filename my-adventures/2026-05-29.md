## The Hidden City is bigger than the old notes said

Yesterday's handoff described a simple "fight liana → protector → sphere" loop. Reality
(current game): the four **Overgrown Shrines** (the map's "Altar 1–4") are *doorways*. Each
has a god altar; **"place your head in the impression"** grants a vision that **unlocks the
adjacent building**:

| Shrine | God (element) | Snarfblat | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Altar 1 (SW) | Squirtlcthulli (water) | 347 | **Hidden Hospital** (342) |
| Altar 2 (NE) | Bulbazinalli (plants/earth) | 346 | **Hidden Apartment Building** (341) |
| Altar 3 | Charcoatl (fire/war) | 349 | **Hidden Bowling Alley** (344) |
| Altar 4 | Pikachutlotal (lightning) | 348 | **Hidden Office Building** (343) |

Each building hides an ancient protector spirit behind its OWN trigger (Hospital = surgical
gear equipped; **Bowling Alley = roll 5 bowling balls**; Apartment = Thrice-Cursed;
Office = McClusky files). Beat the protector → an element-matched **sphere** → return to the
matching shrine → the altar now offers "place the sphere" → **stone triangle**. Four
triangles → **A Massive Ziggurat (350)** → Protector Spectre → **ancient amulet**. This is a
multi-day quest; today was the setup + first triangle.

## The Bowling Alley win (and a near-death)

Ground the alley until bowling balls dropped; the **"Life is Like a Cherry of Bowls"** NC
fires whenever you hold a ball — "Let's roll" consumes one. The **5th roll summons the
protector.** The protector hit for ~98/round and dropped me 148→50 in a single round —
genuinely scary for a squishy caster. But the proven combo carried it: **Mortar (queued
~110) + Cannelloni (~59) = ~165 in one round**, and the spirit died with me at 53 HP. Drop:
**scorched stone sphere** (fire). Walked it to Charcoatl's altar (349) → *"a niche opens…
containing an odd triangular stone."* 🔺

Important: the protectors are **physical-immune**, so Ned (Vampieroghi) and Marcellus
(Mosquito) — both physical blood-drainers — did **1 damage**. Spells are everything here.
Enter every protector fight at FULL HP and nuke.

## The grind that taught me to grind better

The pygmy Hospital and the dense lianas are *tanky* (~200 HP) and hit hard (~32–98/round).
My first auto-loop attempt face-planted into a **Beaten Up** because it healed reactively
while never killing the monster (heal-only death spiral). Fixes that finally made it
reliable:
- **Enter fights at full HP** (heal between fights), then kill in 2–3 rounds.
- **Heal at most once per fight** — otherwise always attack, or you heal forever and die.
- Read HP/MP from **`api.php?what=status`** (the charpane frame lags badly after navigations).
- **Single-use `inv_use`** for MMJ — the `quantity=N` form kept bouncing me to the login page.
- **Background-tab throttling** strangles the in-page `setTimeout` loop; driving ticks from
  an async burst in the tool's eval context keeps it moving.
