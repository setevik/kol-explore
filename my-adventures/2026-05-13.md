## Mechanic Discoveries Day 31

### Pastamastery skill (3006)
- 10 MP, once per day
- Gives +3 dry noodles per cast
- Appears in skills.php list ONLY on days when not yet cast (auto-hides post-cast)
- The "Daily Reminders" at top of main.php tracks remaining daily skills

### Stuffed Mortar Shell (3007)
- 8 MP combat skill, **damage delayed to next round**
- ~101 dmg at Mys 119 buffed (~2x Cannelloni)
- **Cannot stack** — skill disappears from menu while a Mortar is pending
- Always cast Mortar first if available, then Cannelloni

### Twin Peak quest gating (snarfblat 297)
- 4 noncombats, each gated by a different condition:
  1. **Hot resistance** ≥ 4 (need gear)
  2. **Stench resistance** ≥ 4 (need gear)
  3. **Drunkenness check** (some range)
  4. **Library quest done** (Spookyraven prereq)
- Get all 4 stones → signal fire lights → quest complete
- "Search the pantry/shelves" is NOT a stone-laying noncombat, it's a flavor side-encounter

### Castle floor unlock chain
- Basement (322): warning ≥130 Mys, bypass with `&ignorewarning=1` works
- Ground Floor (323): warning ≥130 Mys, bypass with `&ignorewarning=1` works
- Top Floor (324): warning ≥130 Mys, requires CLICKING "I am brave or foolish" button (not URL param)
- Quest line changes per floor: "garbage" → "kitchen counter" → "from above?"

### Cooking ingredient menu (`craft.php?mode=cook`)
- Recipes are gated by what's "discovered" via crafting + class
- Pastamancer noodle recipes need bat wings (Bat Hole) or batgut
