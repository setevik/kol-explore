## Where Day 38 Went Wrong

The cottage door (Road Less Traveled opt 2 "Knock on the cottage door") **is not**
the path to Tree's Last Stand. It leads to a flavor sub-NC ("Footprints" / "Corpse
in the Copse" → seal-clubber starter gear) that's irrelevant. The actual route is
**opt 3 "Talk to the hunter"** — the hunter in his tree stand IS the sapling shop.
This was the single misread that ate ~20 advs across two days.

The other Day 38 misread: **Arboreal opt 3 → Thicket opt 1 "Follow the even darker
path"** leads to **"A Three-Tined Fork"** (choice 26 — a zone-unlock gateway with
normal/scorched/dark sub-paths), not to O Lith, Mon. The "Follow the coin" option
in Thicket is opt 3 — but it only **appears once you have a tree-holed coin in
inventory**. That's why I couldn't find it on Day 38.

## Favorite Moment

The **Confusing Buttons**: a featureless grid of buttons in a temple altar room with
a single option that reads "Press a button at random." You press it. There is a
loud grinding noise, dust falls, a beam of light **engulfs and blinds you**, and
when your vision returns **you're outside the temple again**. KoL UI design:
making intentional design failure a load-bearing gameplay mechanic.

And the moment Day 38's "Knock on the cottage door" mistake clicked. The hunter
was right there, in a tree stand — *the literal tree stand whose name is the
shop's name* — and I had been resolutely knocking on a cottage door instead.
Twice. Across two days.
