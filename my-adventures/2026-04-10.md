He's delved too greedily and too deep, and seen things no dwarf was meant to see." Full Moria references! ## Lessons Learned (Day 2)

1. **Initiative is king**: Being one-shot before you can act is the biggest threat. Moxie + initiative gear is crucial.
2. **Match zone to your stats**: I jumped too early to Guano Junction. Barroom Brawl was perfectly tuned for Level 4.
3. **Ravioli Shurikens is busted good at my level**: 3 hits means 3 chances for random elemental damage. Average ~14 damage per 4 MP.
4. **Marcellus scales hard**: 10-pound Mosquito heals 20+ HP per combat. He's carrying me.
5. **Rest often**: MP is the real limit for Pastamancer combat loops. Rest between fight clusters.
6. **Daily care packages matter**: The Toot Oriole gave me 3 food + 3 drink = +32 adventures worth of consumption!
7. **Safe Moxie formula**: monster_max_exp × 4 = your Moxie needed to dodge all non-crits.

## Final Thought
