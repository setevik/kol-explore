### ⛏️ Mining Breakthrough — Wiki Insight

Per wiki research:
- **Sparkles are random animations**, NOT reward indicators (I'd been avoiding sparkle3 since Day 18, wasting clicks)
- **Ore tiles are at the TOP of grid (rows 0-3)** in **contiguous clusters of 4 tiles**
- **Trapper accepts ANY 3 chunks of ore type** — chrome, asbestos, linoleum all work!

Applied this on Day 19's mine:
- Click (2,3) Promising → **asbestos ore #1** ✓
- Click (1,3) Promising → **asbestos ore #2** ✓ (contiguous!)
- Click (3,3) Promising → stone of eXtreme power (cluster ended)
- Click (2,2) Promising → cave-in -66 HP
- Click (1,2) Promising → **asbestos ore #3** ✓ (cluster wraps to row 2)

**3 asbestos in 5 clicks** instead of 4 caverns of failure. Wiki saved the day.
