### The Automation Challenge

Today highlighted the technical challenges of automating KoL:
- `setInterval` gets killed when frames navigate (KoL's frameset architecture)
- `setTimeout` chains also die during frame loads
- Async `burst()` functions (recursive tick loops) proved most reliable
- The "Adventure Again" text is a LINK not a BUTTON -- caused major stalls until fixed
- Auto-rebuffing adds overhead but keeps damage output consistent
