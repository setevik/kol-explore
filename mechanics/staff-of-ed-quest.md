## Component 1 — Staff of Fats (Palindome / Dr. Awkward)

**CORRECTION (Day 37):** the Spookyraven cellar drops the **Eye of Ed**, not the Staff
of Fats. The actual Staff of Fats comes from the **Palindome**: Mr. Alarm gives the
Mega Gem after you cook a **wet stunt nut stew** (bird rib + lion oil from Whitey's
Grove + stunt nuts from the Palindome). Equip Mega Gem, re-enter the Palindome →
**Dr. Awkward** boss → drops **Staff of Fats** (and the Drowsy Sword).

The wine-cellar / wine-bomb / unstable-fulminate path below is **the old game's lore**
and does not apply to the current cellar (which uses the mortar-dissolving solution
instead — see `spookyraven-ghost-chain.md` Step 8). Keeping the historical wine-bomb
notes below for reference only.

### Historical (pre-revamp) Spookyraven wine-bomb path — DOES NOT APPLY

Route: build a **wine bomb** to blow open a wall in the Haunted Wine Cellar.

1. **Chateau de Vinegar** — fight **wine racks** in the **Haunted Wine Cellar**.
2. **blasting soda** — fight **cabinets** in the **Haunted Laundry Room**.
3. Cook Chateau de Vinegar + blasting soda → **unstable fulminate** (an equippable).
4. Equip the unstable fulminate, fight **haunted boilers** in the **Haunted Boiler Room**.
   - **Run +ML (ideally +100 ML)** so it becomes a **wine bomb** off a single boiler.
5. Use/trigger the wine bomb to blow the cellar wall → reach the lab → **Staff of Fats**.

Shortcut note from search: Spookyraven's spectacles, equipped, reveal a glowing-green
"shortcut" scrawl on the wall list. (Verify in-game.)

Prereq: getting into the Spookyraven cellar at all may need the manor opened (Lights Out /
ghost chain context — see `spookyraven-ghost-chain.md`, though that file is the Lady
Spookyraven side-chain, not the cellar itself). Verify the cellar entrance in-game.

## Component 2 — ancient amulet (Hidden Temple → Hidden City)

1. **Unlock the Hidden Temple** in the Distant Woods — either yourself or via the
   **"Dakota" Fanning** NPC (`place.php?whichplace=woods` → `woods_dakota_anim`).
   - Likely also needs the Spooky Forest "Arboreal Respite" sapling/temple-map chain
     if Dakota's help isn't available. **Verify.**
   - `stone wool` → **Stone-Faced** effect gives a Hidden Temple choice adventure that
     lets you pick the noncombat you need (big time-saver, optional).
2. In the **Hidden Temple**: adventure until **"Such Great Heights"** → choose to climb
   down the vines → **Nostril of the Serpent** (key to enter the Hidden City).
3. **Hidden City** has 4 buildings, each with an **ancient protector spirit**:
   - The Hidden Hospital, The Hidden Bowling Alley, The Hidden Apartment Building,
     The Hidden Office Building.
   - **Protector spirits are IMMUNE to physical damage — need ELEMENTAL damage.**
     (Pastamancer: Cannelloni Cannon is elemental-ish? VERIFY damage type; may need
     spell/elemental source. This is a likely blocker for a pasta-spoon build.)
   - Each spirit drops a **sphere** → take to the **overgrown shrines** → exchange for
     a **stone triangle**. Collect all **4 stone triangles**.
4. With 4 stone triangles → enter **A Massive Ziggurat** → defeat the **Protector
   Spectre** (also physical-immune, needs elemental) → **ancient amulet**.

## Component 3 — Eye of Ed (Spookyraven Manor cellar) ✅ ACQUIRED Day 37

**CORRECTION (verified Day 37):** the Eye of Ed comes from the **Spookyraven cellar**
boss (Lord Spookyraven), NOT the Hidden Temple. See `spookyraven-ghost-chain.md` Step 8
for the full chain (Lady ghost chain → Ballroom "We'll All Be Flat" → cellar opens →
6 mortar-dissolving reagents → wall auto-dissolves → Lord Spookyraven boss → Eye of Ed).

The Hidden Temple is still required, but for **Component 2 (ancient amulet)** — it's the
gateway to the Hidden City.

## The Copperhead Club / Red Zeppelin sub-quest (Shen + Ron)

This is the in-game objective "find Shen / find Ron Copperhead". Relationship to the 3
Staff parts is not 100% clear from search excerpts — treat as its own MacGuffin sub-track
and confirm in-game what it yields (Copperhead Charm + Talisman path).

- **Copperhead Club** is in **The Wrong Side of the Tracks** (unlocked after reading
  father's diary — we've done that).
- First adventure there: meet **Shen Copperhead**; he **poisons you** and demands one
  of these snake artifacts (which one is fixed by the ascension-day you start his quest):
  - Stankara Stone — Batsnake, The Batrat and Ratbat Burrow
  - The First Pizza — Frozen Solid Snake, Lair of the Ninja Snowmen
  - Murphy's Rancid Black Flag — Burning Snake of Fire, Castle Top Floor
  - The Eye of the Stars — The Snake With Like Ten Heads, The Hole in the Sky
  - The Lacrosse Stick of Lacoronado — The Frattlesnake, Smut Orc Logging Camp
  - The Shield of Brook — Snakeleton, The VERY Unquiet Garves
- Return to the club with the item; Shen requests a **2nd** (same pool), poisons again;
  then a **3rd**. After the 3rd you become un-poisoned and get the **Copperhead Charm**.
- **Red Zeppelin**: go to **The Red Zeppelin's Mooring** (The Big Mountains). Must first
  clear **80 protesters** from "A Mob of Zeppelin Protesters" before entering the Red
  Zeppelin (→ Ron Copperhead).

## Pyramid endgame (after Staff of Ed inserted)

Insert Staff of Ed into the pyramid socket (Desert Beach `db_pyramid1`) → it points to
the buried pyramid under Seaside Town. Then:

1. **The Upper Chamber** — boost noncombats; get **"Down Dooby-Doo Down Down"**
   (scheduled) → unlocks **The Middle Chamber**. Also drops **crumbling wooden wheels**.
2. **The Middle Chamber** — +item, sniff a **tomb rat**; get **"Further Down
   Dooby-Doo Down Down"** and **"Under Control"** (both scheduled) → unlock **The Lower
   Chambers** and the **Control Room**. Tomb rats drop **tomb ratchets**.
3. **Control Room**: rotate the lower chambers with the wheel using **crumbling wooden
   wheels** (Upper Chamber noncombat) or **tomb ratchets** (tomb rat post-combat drop).
4. Final boss: **Ed the Undying** (the Holy MacGuffin payoff).

## ⚠️ Pastamancer concern — elemental damage requirement

Hidden City protector spirits and the Protector Spectre are **physical-immune**; they
need **elemental** damage. Our build leans on Cannelloni Cannon / Mortar (verify their
element) + a 1-2 dmg pasta spoon + companions. **Before committing to the Hidden City,
verify we have a reliable elemental damage source** (spell element, or combat items like
photoprotoneutron torpedo ×7, sonar-in-a-biscuit, etc.). This could gate Component 2.
